Free Fish Ministries

Serving fish and faith across Wakulla County

Welcome

Welcome to Free Fish Ministries — a mission founded by Jason Allbaugh and his partner Steven Hooker. What started as a simple fishing trip has grown into a calling from the Lord. We serve hot, fresh fish and share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with anyone willing to listen, learn, and receive God’s love.

Our Mission

We aim to help people overcome drug and alcohol addiction by teaching them how to build a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Through prayer, fellowship, and service, we bring hope and healing in the name of the Lord.

Scripture

"Come, follow me," Jesus said, "and I will send you out to fish for people."
— Matthew 4:19

Where We Serve

We set up in different locations around Wakulla County. One regular spot is Shell Point, Florida — typically once a month on Saturdays and Sundays. The meals are free and so is the message of Jesus. To find out where we'll be next, just call or email us.
